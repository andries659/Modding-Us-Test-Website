Roles in Among Us

Explore different roles in Among Us:

  • Impostor: Deceive and eliminate!
  • Crewmate: Complete tasks and survive.
  • Engineer: Fix critical issues.
  • Scientist: Monitor player vitals.
  • Shapeshifter: Morph into other players.
  • Jester: Try to get voted out, and win the game.
  • Sherrif: Figure out who the Impostor is, and shoot them.
